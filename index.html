Train · Kaelix Vantage

Author cyber courses
that actually land.

A single studio for lab courses on live ranges and content-only training. KAI-assisted framework mapping, version control, and publish workflows — built for instructors, not engineers.

  • Content Studio — structured authoring with confidence-scored NICE/DCWF suggestions.
  • Cohorts — schedule groups through a set of courses with auto-deployed labs.
  • Catalog — framework-mapped courses students can browse and enroll into.
Infrastructure · Kaelix CORE

Sovereign cyber
infrastructure.

CORE is Kaelix's self-hosted infrastructure layer — GitOps-managed and AI-capable, powering every cyber range and student experience. Deploy on-prem, in your cloud, or hybrid. Vantage's labs run straight on CORE.

  • Live deploy queue — concurrent provisioning with stagger control and per-template metrics.
  • Realistic environments — multi-VM enterprise ranges with attack platforms and monitors.
  • Extensible — add a cluster, add a capability. No re-architecting.
Track · KAI Adaptive Intelligence

Proof you can put
in front of leadership.

Every lab, assessment, and submission rolls up into proficiency tracked against work-role frameworks. Score distributions, per-student readiness, and AI-flagged gaps — page-aware and role-filtered across the platform.

  • Assessment analytics — score distribution, pass thresholds, suspicious-item detection.
  • Readiness signals — who's on track, who's at risk, who needs help — automatically.
  • 65+ KAI tools — embedded for students, instructors, and admins alike.
